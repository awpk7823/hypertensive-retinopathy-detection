Hypertensive Retinopathy Detection

In this project, we have implemented an automated HYPERTENSIVE RETINOPATHY detection system. CNN has been used for the identification and segmentation of arteries and veins. The AVR ratio, which is a measure to identify hypertensive retinopathy, has been calculated by image processing using OpenCV.

ABOUT CDAC

Center for Development of Advanced Computing, Mohali

Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (C-DAC) is the premier R&D organization of the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) for carrying out R&D in IT, Electronics and associated areas.
